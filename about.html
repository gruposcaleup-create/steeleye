About us

Built for steel contractors. Powered by AI.

SteelEye was created to solve a real industry problem: slow takeoffs, costly errors, and fragmented workflows. We partnered directly with contractors to build a platform that's faster, more accurate, and fully unified.

Our mission is to rapidly simplify estimating and restore productivity to your team.

Our Mission

To modernize and accelerate the steel industry with smart, simple, and accurate technology.

Our Story

SteelEye started as an internal solution developed in Alabama. After seeing thousands of hours wasted in repetitive takeoff work, our team decided to build a platform that's faster, more accurate, and fully unified.

Partner

Developed Side-by-Side with Contrerras Steel

SteelEye was built and validated through real projects in direct collaboration with Contrerras Steel ensuring a product truly aligned with industry needs.
